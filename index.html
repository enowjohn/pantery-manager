Daily Hydration

Water Intake Guidelines

💧 Men: 3.7L (15.5 cups)
💧 Women: 2.7L (11.5 cups)
🏃 Add 1.5L per hour of exercise

Hydration Tips

  • Drink water before, during, and after meals
  • Keep a water bottle visible throughout the day
  • Eat water-rich foods (cucumber, watermelon)
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol intake
  • Monitor urine color (pale yellow = hydrated)

About Pantry Manager

Our mission to reduce food waste globally

Founded in 2026, Pantry Manager emerged from a simple observation: too much good food goes to waste while families struggle with rising grocery costs.

Our team combines expertise in supply chain management, user experience design, and artificial intelligence to create solutions that make a real difference in people's daily lives.

We believe that technology should help us live more sustainably while saving money and reducing stress around meal planning.
